# Netflix_Content_Strategy_Analysis
Data analysis project to provide strategic recommendations for netflix content planning
Netflix Content Strategy Analysis
Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze Netflix’s content dataset and provide data-driven strategic recommendations for future content planning.

This analysis focuses on:
Comparing Movies vs TV Shows distribution
Identifying the most common genres
Analyzing content release trends by year
Providing strategic recommendations based on insights

Tools & Technologies Used
Python
Pandas
Matplotlib
Seaborn

Jupyter Notebook

Analysis Performed
1. Movies vs TV Shows Distribution
Analyzed the proportion of Movies and TV Shows to understand Netflix’s content mix and platform focus.
2. Content Release Trend by Year
Evaluated yearly release patterns to identify growth trends and peak production years.
3. Genre Analysis
Identified the most common genres to understand audience demand and content strategy.

Key Insights
Movies form a major portion of the total content library.
Drama and Action are among the most dominant genres.
Content production increased steadily over the years before reaching a peak.
TV Shows demonstrate potential for improving long-term user engagement.
Strategic Recommendations

Based on the analysis:
Netflix should continue investing in high-performing genres such as Drama and Action.
Increasing focus on TV Shows can improve long-term subscriber retention.
Content diversification into emerging genres can help attract niche audiences.
Maintaining consistent yearly content releases will sustain growth momentum.
